PSG's Squad

PSG's squad is worth almost one billion euros. In total, PSG have 33 first team players on the books as Ligue 1. With an estimated market value totalling 998.95 million euros. Fuelled by their Qatari ownership, the club have been able to compete with the likes of Manchester City in spending astronomical sums of money on some of the best players in Europe for nearly a decade, but their current squad now resembles a French reincarnation of the Real Madrid Galactico era. Mauricio Pochettino’s side have landed multiple key signings this summer, with the additions of three high-profile free transfers in Italy goalkeeper Gianluigi Donnarumma from AC Milan, Sergio Ramos from Real Madrid,Georginio Wijnaldum from Liverpool and the best player in the world Leo Messi From FC Barcalona.
